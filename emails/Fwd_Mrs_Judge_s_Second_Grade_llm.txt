Summary of the School Letter

The letter from Mrs. Judge provides an update on the first week of second grade at Fulton Science Academy. Students are adjusting well to new routines and are excited about the upcoming playground. Parents are encouraged to use Class Dojo to monitor their child's behavior and see classroom updates. Important reminders include sending in the Friday folder weekly, reviewing student work at home, and being aware of dismissal and club procedures. Several upcoming events are listed, and the school requests support in voting for the Best K-12 Private School award. Additional notes cover lunch ordering, classroom wish lists, and nut allergy precautions.
